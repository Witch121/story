An elf is a mythical creature that is often depicted as a humanoid with long ears and a slender, graceful appearance. 
In folklore and fantasy literature, elves are known for their longevity, beauty, and affinity for nature. They are commonly portrayed 
as skilled archers, magic users, and inhabitants of forests or hidden realms. Elves are often associated with wisdom, elegance, and a deep 
connection to the natural world.
